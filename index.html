Data Science Projects

Population Census Project

The main goal of this project is to uncover insights and hidden patterns in the data and use them to advise on the most appropriate services to be invested in and also recommend the best facility to be built on a large piece of land. The data is first preprocessed and properly formatted to ensure misleading information and errors in the data are eliminated.

Customer Data Preprocessing and formatting

This project focuses on data preprocessing, aggregation, transformation, formatting and storage tasks on a customer data stored in a CSV format. The main objective is to prepare, preprocess and treat data errors to aid visualization and analysis and also create insights for decision making.

GSK Clinical Trial Data Analysis

This projects examines a clinical trial data from GSK pharmaceuticals to determine which subgroups of patients benefits more from two types of treatments. It also identifies which attributes of patients will determine their response to treatment and also build a model to predict a patient's response to treatment.

Bank Credit Card Project

The main objective of this project is to group customers based on their account and credit card details using unsupervised machine learning and also split the customers into groups (clusters) based on their means of contacting the bank's customer service. For this clustering project, the data is first cleaned, preprocessed and treated for errors.

Personal Loan Project

This project's main goal is to provide valuable insights for a bank to determine which which goups of customers took Loan and also develop a supervised ML model to predict whether a customer will likely accept a loan. My proposed model had a 90% accuracy and 100% recall, effectively predicting if a customer will take a loan or not.

The project is aimed at developing an AI algorithm to help determine the presence or absence of brain tumor from MRI scans. Image processing and resizing were carried out. The final model accurately predicted brain tumors with 99% accuracies. CNN, VGG-16 and SVM algorithms were implemented and evaluated.
